How Food Bracket Works

  1. The app starts empty. Add at least two foods to start a fair tournament.
  2. Use Quick add for common options, or type your own food into the input.
  3. Each matchup asks one question: which food would you rather eat right now?
  4. Foods with multiple styles can be customized before you start picking.
  5. The winner of a matchup advances to the next round.
  6. You can change a pick by clicking another food in that matchup.
  7. Shuffle randomizes the starting order. Reset clears all picks but keeps the food list.
  8. Color presets and wallpapers only change the look of the page.
  9. When one food wins the final, it becomes the champion.
